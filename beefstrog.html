Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Flour
  • Broth
  • Sour cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Slice beef into thin strips and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter and sear beef quickly, then remove.
  3. Cook onions until soft, then add mushrooms.
  4. Sprinkle flour and stir for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Slowly add broth while stirring into a sauce.
  6. Return beef and simmer 5-10 minutes.
  7. Reduce heat and stir in sour cream (do not boil).
  8. Adjust seasoning and serve immediately.
