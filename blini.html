Blini

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Butter/oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, sugar, and salt until fully combined.
  2. Gradually add flour while whisking to avoid lumps until batter is smooth and slightly thin.
  3. Stir in melted butter and let the batter rest for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan and tilt immediately to spread thinly.
  6. Cook 1-2 minutes until edges lift and bottom is lightly golden.
  7. Flip carefully and cook the other side for 30-60 seconds.
  8. Transfer to a plate and repeat, stacking blini as you go.
  9. Serve warm with sour cream, jam, or other toppings.
