Borscht

Ingredients

  • 2 beets, grated
  • 1/2 head cabbage, shredded
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 6 cups broth
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper
  • Sour cream, dill

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and sauté chopped onion for 3-4 minutes until soft.
  2. Add grated carrot and beets and cook 5-7 minutes to develop flavor.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in broth and bring to a gentle boil.
  5. Add potatoes and cabbage, then reduce heat to a simmer.
  6. Cook 20-30 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  7. Add garlic and vinegar or lemon juice to balance flavor.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot with sour cream and fresh dill.
