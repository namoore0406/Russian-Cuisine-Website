Olivier Salad

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Eggs
  • Pickles
  • Meat
  • Peas
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes and carrots until tender, then cool completely.
  2. Dice potatoes, carrots, eggs, pickles, and meat evenly.
  3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl with peas.
  4. Add mayonnaise gradually and mix evenly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
