Pelimi

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt
  • Filling
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, egg, water, and salt to form dough.
  2. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Mix filling ingredients thoroughly in a bowl.
  5. Roll dough thin (about 1-2 mm thick).
  6. Cut circles using a glass or cutter.
  7. Place a small amount of filling in each circle.
  8. Fold, pinch edges tightly, and connect corners.
  9. Boil salted water and add dumplings.
  10. Cook until they float, then 3-4 minutes more.
  11. Remove with slotted spoon and serve immediately.
