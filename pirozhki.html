Pirozhki

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Filling (vegetables or meat)

Instructions

  1. Warm milk and dissolve yeast and sugar. Let sit 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add egg, flour, and salt, knead into soft dough.
  3. Let dough rise for about 1 hour.
  4. Prepare filling by cooking meat or vegetables.
  5. Divide dough into portions and flatten each piece.
  6. Add filling, fold, and seal edges tightly.
  7. Place on baking sheet or prepare for frying.
  8. Bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes or fry until golden.
  9. Let cool slightly before serving.
